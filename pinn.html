Simulation of fluid dynamics using Physics-Informed Neural Networks

My master's thesis is on physics-informed neural networks. Fluid mechanics is a very demanding area of physics. The mathematical description of fluid flow consists of complex partial differential equations that do not have analytical solutions. To conduct a simulation, numerical solutions must be used, which are computationally expensive.
