Simulation of galaxy collisions

This is numeric simulation of two colliding galaxies. The program is very basic in terms of optimization.
I have applied the simplest numerical algorithm to solve gravitational field equations, but it calculates "stars" and "galaxies" position in real-time.
Graphic was done with PyGame library. It was hard to gain some good efficiency (Python is so sloooow) and to achieve good FPS ,with at least 2000 stars, I used NumPy arrays.
Above we can see screenshot of running program with two colliding galaxies vs two real galaxies colliding. I think there is some kind of similarity between them :)
